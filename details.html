Pure Concept Design Architect

We're looking for a Concept Design Architect for one of the Middle East's most influential Architects on a high-end high-rise mixed use/hospitality project.

We're looking for someone who is heavily focused on design to work on two high-rise projects. High-rise experience is advantageous, but not essential as someone with this amount of experience should be more than capable. You are able to produce high-end, creative design work on high profile buildings throughout the Middle East.

Salaries are up to AED 40k per month, tax free for the right background.
